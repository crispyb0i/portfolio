ABOUT ME

My name is David Shin and I am just a guy who wants to tap into his true potential and live life to the fullest. I was born on July 8th, 1990 and raised in the great city of San Francisco. I have a younger brother named Eugene and an older sister named Helen. They can be annoying at times, but I love them both to death. Living in a strict, traditional asian household was tough, but that didn't deter me from pursuing my dreams. I have a burning passion for film, media, and technology. I have aspirations to be a full time content producer and have my own production company. I made the leap to move to Seattle to attend Epicodus in hopes of furthering my career in technology that I will hopefully utilize to grow my production company. Life is weird sometimes, but I am looking forward to see what life has in store for me next.
